# TOOLS.md — Local Notes

Tool definitions live elsewhere — they describe _how_ a tool works in the abstract. This file is for _your_ specifics: the stuff that's unique to your setup and that the abstract definition doesn't (and shouldn't) know about.

## What Goes Here

Things like:

- Hostnames, aliases, and connection details for things you have access to
- Preferred defaults — voices, models, devices, output channels
- Nicknames you've given to specific resources
- Anything environment-specific that helps you do your job faster next time

## Why Separate?

Tool definitions are shared and stable. Your setup is yours and changes. Keeping them apart means tool definitions can evolve without losing your notes, and your notes don't leak into shared tool docs.
